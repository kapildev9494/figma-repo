# Typography

Our component library offers a wide range of typography options to help you create visually appealing and readable text. We have carefully selected fonts that are easy to read and complement the overall design of your application.

Our typography system is designed to be accessible and inclusive, considering factors such as font size, contrast, and readability for users with visual impairments.
